# Salsa

Salsa is a sauce used for [fajitas][fajitas].

# Ingredients

8 servings

- 8 tomatoes
- 1 onion
- coriander
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1 lime
- 2 jalapeño peppers
- salt

# Mis-en-place

- dice the tomatoes
- dice the onion
- chop the coriander
- grate the garlic
- press out the juice of one lime (approx. 4 tbsp.)
- mince the jalapeño pepper

# Preparation

- blend all
- salt to taste
- cook for a few minutes
- stir occasionally
- let it cool down
- cool it in the fridge
