# Salsa

Salsa is a sauce used for [fajitas][fajitas].

## Ingredients

8 servings

- 8 tomatoes
- 1 onion
- 12 g coriander
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1-4 limes (4 tbsp lime juice)
- 2 jalapeño peppers
- salt

## Mis-en-place

- dice the tomatoes
- dice the onion
- chop the coriander
- grate the garlic
- press out approx. 4 tbsp juice of the limes
- mince the jalapeño peppers

## Preparation

- mix:
    - tomatoes
    - onion
    - coriander
    - garlic
    - lime juice
    - jalapeños
- salt to taste
- cook for a few minutes
- stir occasionally
- let it cool down
- cool it in the fridge
