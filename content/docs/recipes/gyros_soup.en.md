The amount of water is an approximate. Use more, or less, depending on how think you want the soup to be.

## Marinade

- [gyros seasoning][gyrosspice]
- olivove oil
- onion rings (½-rings, ¼-rings)
- sald


## Mise-en-place

- marinate the meat one day in advance --- or order already marinated gyros meat at your butcher
- cut the onion (semi-coarse)
- dice the potatoes
- grate the garlic
- dice the red pepper
- dice the green pepper


## Preparation

{{% steps %}}
1. ### Fry

    - fry meat on fat, heavy heat, put aside, let cool down
    - fry onion and garlic, deglaze with water

1. ### Cook onion, potatoes

    - add some more water
    - cook onion (onion soup it the base)
    - add potatoes
    - add seasoning
    - cook until the potatoes are cooked
    - while cooking, cut fried gyros meat to dice

1. ### Cook and purée vegetables

    - add passata
    - add ¾ of the cut bell peppers
    - cook until bell peppers are soft
    - purée to 60-80%

1. ### Meat and Finish

    - add fried gyros meat and cook a bit
    - add the rest of the bell peppers
    - stir in the cream
    - dilute processed cheese in warm water and stir it in
    - salt and finalize the taste with red wine vinegar
    - let cool down and rest, let taste develop further
{{% /steps %}}
