# Cajun Spice Mix

Spice mix used for [jambalaya][jambalaya].

## Ingredients

- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- ½ tsp thyme
- ½ tsp oregano
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- ½ tsp pepper
- ½ tsp salt
- chilli pepper
- cumin

## Preparation

- mix all
