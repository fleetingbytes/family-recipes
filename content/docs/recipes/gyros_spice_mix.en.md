# Gyros Seasoning

- 6 tsp oregano (dryed)
- 4 tsp thyme (dryen)
- 2 tsp majoram (dryed)
- 2 tsp rosemary (dryed)
- 2 tsp coriander (dryed, ground)
- 1 tsp cumin (ground)
- 1 tsp chilli pepper (ground) (only Â½ a spoon if it is too spicy)

- 3 tsp peppecorn (ground)
- 3 tsp salt
