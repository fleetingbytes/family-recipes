# Jambalaya

Traditional meal from Louisiana.

Obligatory music: Jambalaya (Hank Williams, 1951).

## Ingredients

- 800 g chicken filet
- 2 onions
- 2 red bell pepper
- basmati rice
- (stick) celery
- garlic
- fat
- olive oil
- tomato paste
- chicken stock
- [Cajun spice mix][cajunspice]

## Mis-en-place

- grate the garlic
- cut and [marinade](#marinade) the chicken
- cut bell pepper
- cut celery
- soak rice (20 min max., then drain)

## Marinade

- olive oil
- ⅔ of the grated garlic
- ½ of the cajun spice
- salt

## Preparation

{{% steps %}}
1. ### Fry Chicken

    - fry onion on fat
    - add ⅓ of the grated garlic
    - add and fry chicken
    - put chicken aside

2. ### Fry Vegetables

    - add bell pepper (cover)
    - add celery (cover)
    - deglaze with stock
    - cook

3. ### Cook Rice

    - add rice (drained)
    - add stock
    - add tomato paste
    - add ½ of the cajun spice
    - cook until rice is cooked
    - gradualy add stock of water if it gets too thick

4. ### Finish

    - cook to reach desired thickness
    - salt to taste
    - put back the chicken to warm it back up
{{% /steps %}}
