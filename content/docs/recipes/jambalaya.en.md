# Jambalaya

Traditional meal from Luisiana.

Obligatory music: Jambalaya (Hank Williams, 1951).

## Ingredients

- 800 g chicken filet
- 2 onions
- 2 red bell pepper
- basmati rice
- (stick) celery
- garlic
- fat
- olive oil
- tomato paste
- chicken stock
- [Cajun spice mix][cajunspice]

## Marinade

- olive oil
- ⅔ of the grated garlic
- ½ of the cajun spice
- salt

## Mis-en-place

- cut and marinade the chicken
- cut bell pepper
- cut celery

## Preparation

### Fry Chicken

- fry onion on fat
- add ⅓ of the grated garlic
- add and fry chicken
- put chicken aside

### Fry Vegetables

- add bell pepper
- add celery
- deglaze with stock

### Cook Rice

- add rice
- add stock
- add tomato paste
- add ½ of the cajun spice
- cook until rice is cooked
- gradualy add stock of water if it gets too thick

### Finish

- cook to reach desired thickness
- salt to taste
- put back the chicken to warm it back up
