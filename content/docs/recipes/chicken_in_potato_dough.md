# Chicken in Potato Dough

## Ingredients

{{< tabs "ingredients for chicken in potato dough" >}}
{{% tab "5 servings" %}}
- 3 eggs
- 150 g yoghurt
- 2 spoons of parsely (chopped)
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 100 g cheese
- 3 medium-sized potatoes
- 400 g chicken breasts
- chicken seasoning
- 80 g flour
- fat for frying
{{% /tab %}}
{{< /tabs >}}

### Chicken Seasoning

- 1 tsp paprika
- 3 g salt
- ½ tsp curry
- ¼ tsp cayenne pepper

## Mise-en-place
- chop the parsley
- grate the garlic
- grate the cheese
- grate the potatoes
- dice the chicken breasts, mix with seasoning
- sieve the flour

## Preparation

### Dough

- beat the eggs
- add yoghurt
- add parsley
- add garlic
- add cheese
- add potatoes
- add meat
- salt and pepper
- put in the fridge to rest (15--30 min)

### Fry

- on heated fat on the pan, form palm-sized cakes with a spoon
- fry until done and golden
